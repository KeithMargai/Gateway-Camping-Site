Events at Gateway Camping Site

Hiking

Enjoy for a beautiful hiking experience to multiple pathways to check out and explore.

Fishing

Enjoy our fishing spot on our vast and clean lake. Good spot to teach your young ones to fish.

Canoeing

If you love Canoeing, we have competitions for a canoe race at another lake on the camping site, the group that wins a special prize at the end. The canoe racing are only on Mondays and Fridays
